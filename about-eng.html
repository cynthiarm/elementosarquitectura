Who we are

We are a Mexican company dedicated to the development of Architecture; Design, Planning and Construction, our approach is to take the client by the hand from his first ideas to see his dreams materialized, accompanying him throughout the process. Our inception was born as a response to the observation of urban, design and construction problems, resulting in the search to provide a professional and punctual service in every aspect of our work.



A little bit of History

Elementos Arquitectura was founded in 2017 by two architects graduated from the Universidad Autónoma de Baja California. The Architect Abraham Souza, begins his professional work in the city of Mexicali B.C. developing his extensive experience in different works, both public and private. Architect Daniel Quiles, has stood out for his architectural quality in the development of residential and commercial projects in Puerto Peñasco, Son. They decided to match their experiences with collaborations in 2016 resulting in the idea of generating a union, which later led them to the formalization of what is today Elementos Arquitectura.
